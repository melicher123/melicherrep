About Us

Welcome to LawnLuxe! We are a proud Slovak company dedicated to providing top-notch lawn care services to keep your lawn looking its best year-round. Founded by two brothers following in the footsteps of our father's business, we aim to exceed the high standards he set and continue the family tradition of excellence in lawn care.

Although we are relatively new to the market, having been in operation for just two years, we have successfully completed dozens of projects. Our long-term experience and practice in lawn establishment are your guarantee of quality. We use only the best equipment and tools to ensure that every job is done to the highest standard.

Our services are tailored to meet the needs of various clients, including homeowners, businesses, builders, and gardeners. Whether you need regular maintenance, new lawn establishment, or specialized care, we are here to help. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has quickly earned us a reputation for reliability and quality.

We believe in the importance of continuous support and long-term maintenance. Establishing a lawn is just the beginning; maintaining it in top condition is essential. We provide comprehensive services that ensure your lawn remains healthy and beautiful throughout the year.

If you are looking for expert advice, we are happy to help. Feel free to ask us for advice if you are uncertain. Send us photos of your lawn, and we will provide tailored recommendations to ensure your lawn thrives.
